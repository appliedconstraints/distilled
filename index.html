Distilled

Distilled is an email of five hand picked links that will help you learn the business of software, delivered every two weeks.

Starting or growing a software business takes a lot of time. Between designing, developing, marketing, and supporting your business - there's a lot to do and a lot to learn.

The web is a great place to learn, but who's got the time to sift through all the blogs, videos, magazines and other resources out there? With Distilled, you don't have to.
